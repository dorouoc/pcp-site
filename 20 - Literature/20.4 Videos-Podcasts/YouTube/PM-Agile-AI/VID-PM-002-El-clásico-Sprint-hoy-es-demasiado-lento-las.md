# 🎬 El clásico Sprint hoy es demasiado lento: las 3 velocidades de los equipos con IA

**Channel**: Javier Garzás  
**URL**: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nOtaMh2yRfI  
**Relevance**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

## Summary

Garzás argues that AI-enabled teams operate at three simultaneous velocities: (1) Instant velocity — AI completes routine tasks in minutes rather than days, (2) Sprint velocity — human-AI collaboration completes features in hours rather than weeks, (3) Strategic velocity — AI-generated insights enable real-time strategic pivots. Traditional 2-week Scrum sprints are misaligned with all three.

---

## PUMA Relevance

The 'three velocities' framework is directly relevant to PUMA's motivation: PUMA's automated triage and estimation operate at Instant velocity, reducing the PM overhead that previously occurred at Sprint velocity. This supports PUMA's argument that AI automation does not replace PMs but elevates them to strategic roles.
