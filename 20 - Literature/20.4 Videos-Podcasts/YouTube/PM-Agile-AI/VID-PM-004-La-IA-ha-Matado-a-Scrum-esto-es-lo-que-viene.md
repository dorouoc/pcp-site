# 🎬 La IA ha Matado a Scrum (esto es lo que viene Ahora)

**Channel**: Javier Garzás  
**URL**: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jrTrihcZZlc  
**Relevance**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

## Summary

Garzás argues that Scrum's ceremony-heavy structure is incompatible with AI-assisted development. When AI can complete in 2 hours what previously took a sprint, the 2-week sprint boundary becomes artificial. Proposes continuous flow with AI-assisted backlog management as the replacement.

---

## PUMA Relevance

Supports PUMA's contextualisation: the Scrum-based metrics PUMA benchmarks on (sprint velocity, story point estimation) are themselves evolving. PUMA's automated estimation module is relevant not just for traditional Scrum but for the emerging continuous flow models Garzás describes.
