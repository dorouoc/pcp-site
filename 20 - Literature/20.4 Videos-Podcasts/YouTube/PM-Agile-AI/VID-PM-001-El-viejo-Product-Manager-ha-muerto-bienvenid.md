## Summary

Javier Garzás — Spain's most prominent Agile thought leader — argues that the traditional Product Manager role is being fundamentally transformed by AI. The AI PM: automated backlog triage and prioritisation (AI handles routine classification), data-driven sprint planning (AI analyses velocity and risk), and human PM focusing on stakeholder management and strategic decisions.
