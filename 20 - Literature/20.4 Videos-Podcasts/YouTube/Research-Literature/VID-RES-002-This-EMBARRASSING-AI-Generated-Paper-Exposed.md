## Summary

Andy Stapleton analyses a publicly embarrassing case where a major AI company's research paper was found to contain hallucinated references — papers that don't exist but look real. The video explains how AI tools (including ChatGPT, Gemini) fabricate plausible-sounding citations, why this is happening at scale, and how to protect research integrity.
