# 🎬 I Compared Every Popular AI Literature Review Tool So You Don't Have To

**Relevance**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

---

## Summary

Head-to-head comparison of 8 AI literature review tools: Elicit, Consensus, Research Rabbit, Connected Papers, Litmaps, Semantic Scholar, Perplexity, and Scite. Each is tested on the same research question. Evaluation criteria: coverage, accuracy, hallucination rate, citation format, and ease of use.

---

## PUMA Relevance

Justifies PUMA's multi-tool research stack (not relying on a single tool). The comparison shows that no single tool covers all dimensions: Semantic Scholar excels at citation network, Elicit at data extraction, Consensus at evidence quality, Research Rabbit at discovery. PUMA uses all four for complementary coverage.
