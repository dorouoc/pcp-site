## Summary

Complete tutorial on Research Rabbit: how to seed the system with 2–3 known papers and let it discover the surrounding literature via co-citation and semantic similarity. Shows the graph visualisation of the PUMA-relevant PM+LLM cluster, how to export to Zotero, and how to set up weekly alerts.

---

## PUMA Relevance

Research Rabbit was used in PUMA's initial SLR to discover papers related to Cinkusz 2025 and Assalaarachchi 2026 — the direct predecessors that PUMA builds on. The co-citation approach revealed 12 additional relevant papers that keyword search missed. The Zotero export feeds directly into [[60 - Resources/65 Bibliographies/BIB-Master-APA7]].
