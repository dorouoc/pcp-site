## Summary

Systematic, step-by-step guide to conducting a stress-free academic literature review. Covers: defining the search scope with PICO/SPIDER frameworks, systematic database searching, deduplication, screening by title/abstract/full-text, quality assessment using PRISMA, and synthesis. Andy Stapleton is a PhD holder with 100k+ subscribers dedicated to academic research tools.
