## Summary

Spanish-dubbed presentation by Andrew Ng explaining agentic workflows: reflection, tool use, planning, and multi-agent collaboration. Ng argues that iterative agentic loops with feedback dramatically outperform single-pass generation, even with weaker models. The video includes live demonstrations of each pattern.

---

## PUMA Relevance

Foundational theoretical grounding for PUMA's experimental design. Ng's 'agentic loop outperforms single-pass' claim is what PUMA's Stage 4 (ReAct + RAG) tests against Stage 1 (single-pass few-shot). The four workflow types (reflection, tool-use, planning, multi-agent) map to PUMA's four experiment strategies.
