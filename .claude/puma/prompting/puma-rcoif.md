# RCOIF FRAMEWORK

- **Role**: Define who the AI is (e.g., "You are a research assistant.").
- **Context**: Provide background or relevant details.
- **Objective**: State the goal clearly.
- **Instructions**: Step-by-step guidelines for the task.
- **Format**: Specify the expected answer format (bullet list, JSON, etc.).

## USAGE

- Start prompts with Role and Context.
- Clearly outline the Objective and any specific Instructions.
- Provide examples or template to enforce Format if needed.
