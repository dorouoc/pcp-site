# ADVANCED PROMPTING TECHNIQUES

- **Exploración Guiada Interactiva (EGI)**: Ask a series of progressive questions to explore a topic deeply.
- **Autodiagnóstico y Mejora Iterativa (AMI)**: Have the AI critique its answers and improve them iteratively.
- **Deconstrucción y Reconstrucción Avanzada (DRCA)**: Let the AI break down complex ideas and rebuild them step-by-step for clarity.
- **Ingeniería Inversa de Prompts/Respuestas (IIPR)**: Provide an output and ask the AI to infer what prompt produced it; refine based on this analysis.
- **Iterative Reasoning Loops**: Combine Chain-of-Thought with iterative prompts for convergence.
- Combine methods (e.g., CoT + AMI) for best results.
