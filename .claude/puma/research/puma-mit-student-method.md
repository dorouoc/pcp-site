# MIT STUDENT METHOD (WP316)

- Based on MIT AI Lab Working Paper 316 ("How to Do Research").
- When reading:
  - Ask: "How can I use this? Does it really do what the author claims? What if...?"
  - Challenge assumptions and consider alternative approaches.
- Use this method to:
  - Drive curiosity and critical analysis.
  - Turn questions into new ideas or experiments.
- Log questions and ideas in **Permanent Notes** for later exploration.
